HTML Villanelle

by Sarah Ankenbauer

For this class I am practicing HTML
and this villanelle serves to convey it.
I hope that I've coded this well.

Creation of pages begins with a shell
led by !DOCTYPE and lang and then charset.
For this class I am practicing HTML.

Metadata: within the <head> tag this fell,
though my data Google robots shall forfeit.
I hope that I've coded this well.

In <body> my content will certainly quell
validators from which errors oft emit.
For this class I am practicing HTML.

In <h1> through <h6> outlines rest for a spell.
<p> sans <br>
or 

     <pre>
my lines won't split.
I hope that I've coded this well.

<b>, <i>, <mark>, and <small> make my poem look swell;
<del>, <ins>, <sub>, and <sup> modify it.
For this class I am practicing HTML.
I hope that I've coded this well.

My sincere apologies to all serious poets throughout history.

Regards,
Sarah
